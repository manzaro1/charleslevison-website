The Artist

Born and raised along the shores of Lake Malawi in Mangochi, Charles Levison brings a unique perspective to contemporary African art. At 31, he balances his practice between teaching, filmmaking, and painting.

His impressionistic style captures the interplay of light on water, the vibrant colors of local markets, and the resilience of Malawian communities.
